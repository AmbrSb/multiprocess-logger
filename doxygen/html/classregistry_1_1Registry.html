Detailed Description

template<typename C>
class registry::Registry< C >

Maintains a list of RegItem.

Registry is responsible for maintaining a list of RegItem. It exposes its services through a channel defined by C.

Template Parameters
CThis is a channel that exposes to its clients the services of Registry in any way it desires.
